DejaVu Sans and DejaVu Sans Bold are bundled here so the image renderer
works identically on every operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux, and
CI runners) without depending on system-installed fonts.

DejaVu fonts are based on the Bitstream Vera fonts and are released under
a permissive license that explicitly allows redistribution and bundling.
Full license text: https://dejavu-fonts.github.io/License.html
